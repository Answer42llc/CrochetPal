Mouse Cat Toy Crochet Pattern

I made this tiny mouse for my cat and she loved it.

Be sure to subscribe for more adorable crochet ideas.

Materials

4.0 mm hook

Cotton yarn

Body

Round 1: In a MR, sc 6. (6)

Round 2: (sc 2, inc) x 3. (12)

Assembly

Sew the tail and ears to the body.
