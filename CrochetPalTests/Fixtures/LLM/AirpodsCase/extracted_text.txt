You will need:
* DK yarn in the colour(s) of your choice
* A 3mm crochet hook
* A stitch marker
* A darning needle
* A button if you aren’t going to crochet one
Key:
CH: Chain
HTC: Half Treble Crochet
DC: Double Crochet
FO: Fasten Off.
Notes:
The main body of the case is worked in rounds, once you reach the flap section you will
work in rows.
Special Skills:
Magic ring.
***
Body:
CH 12
Starting in the 2nd chain from the hook and working in one
side of the chain, work as follows,
Round 1: 2 HTC in first stitch, 1 HTC in each of the next 8
stitches, 2HTC in the next stitch, working in the other side of
the chain, 2 HTC in the next stitch, 1HTC in each of the next 8
stitches, 2 HTC in the last stitch, join to the 1st HTC. You
should have 24 stitches in total. Mark the beginning of your
round with a stitch marker.
Round 2-6: 1 HTC in each stitch all the way around (24)
Round 7: 1 DC in each stitch all the way around (24)
***
Now we will begin to work the flap, from here on we will be
working in rows back and forth.
Row 1: 1DC, 1DC, CH 2, turn, 1 HTC in the next 10 stitches, CH
2, turn. (10)
Row 2-9: 1 HTC in each stitch all the way across, CH 2, turn (10)
Now we will work our button hole.
Row 10: 2HTCtog, 1HTC, 1HTC, CH2, skip the next 2 stitches, 1HTC, 1HTC, 2HTCtog, CH2,
turn (8)
Row 11: 1HTC in each stitch across, FO. (8)
Button:
Start with a magic ring,
Round 1: work 12 treble crochet, join. (12)
Round 2: SS into each stitch around (12)
Line the button up with where the button hole on the flap lies
when in position at the front of the pouch, (you can mark this
with a stitch marker) and sew into place.
Weave in the ends.
And you’re done!
