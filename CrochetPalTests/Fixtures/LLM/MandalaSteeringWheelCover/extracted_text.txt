Mandala Steering Wheel Cover
Materials:
1 skein of Mandala yarn of your choice
Crochet hook size G or 4.25 mm
Ch 17
Row 1: Sc in 2nd ch from hook and in next 2 chs. Hdc in next ch, dc in next 8 chains. Hdc in
next ch. Sc in last 3 chains. Turn (16)
Rows 2-109: Ch 1, sc in first 3 sts. Hdc in next st. dc in next 8 sts. Hdc in next st. sc in last 3
sts. Finish off and sew the 2 ends together. Weave in all ends.
