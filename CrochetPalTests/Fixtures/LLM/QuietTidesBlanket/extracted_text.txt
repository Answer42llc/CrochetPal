Level of difficulty
Finished Measurements
34" x 28.5" after blocking.
Blocking is highly recommended.
To block: drench blanket in cool water, roll in a towel to get out excess water, stretch and pin into place on a blocking mat or thick towel.
Materials
Crochet Hook size I/5.5mm
Circulo's Alpaya Yarn - 3 hanks of off white, 2 hanks of teal, 2 hanks of light blue
Scissors
Yarn needle
Blocking mat or thick towel and pins
Caring Instructions
The Alpaya yarn is a blend of 70% organic pima cotton and 30% baby alpaca. Hand wash or machine wash in cool water on delicate using a wool safe detergent, then let air dry. Do not put in dryer.
Note: If you would like to make this blanket larger or smaller, just make a foundation ch of a multiple of 3 (+ 3)
Pattern Instructions
With off white, ch 114
Row 1: 1 sc in the 2nd ch from the hook and in each ch across. (113 sc) Ch 1, turn.
Row 2: 1 sc in each sc across, changing to teal in the last sc. Ch 1, turn.
Row 3: 1 sc in each sc across. Ch 1, turn.
Row 4: 1 sc in each sc across, changing to off white in the last sc. Ch 1, turn.
Row 5: 1 sc in the first 2 sc,*fpdc around the next sc from 3 rows below (see reference image), then - back on your working row - sk the sc behind the fpdc you just made, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sc;* repeat from * to * across. Ch 1, turn.
Row 6: 1 sc in each st across, changing to light blue in the last sc. Ch 1, turn.
Row 7: 1 sc in each sc across. Ch 1, turn.
Row 8: 1 sc in each sc across, changing to off white in the last sc. Ch 1, turn.
Row 9: 1 sc in the first 2 sc,*fpdc around the top of the fpdc from 4 rows below, then - back on your working row - sk the sc behind the fpdc you just made, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sc;* repeat from * to * across. Ch 1, turn.
Row 10: 1 sc in each sc across,changing to teal in the last sc. Ch 1, turn.
Row 11: 1 sc in each sc across. Ch 1, turn.
Row 12: 1 sc in each sc across, changing to off white in the last sc. Ch 1, turn.
Row 13: 1 sc in the first 2 sc,*fpdc around the top of the fpdc from 4 rows below, then - back on your working row - sk the sc behind the fpdc you just made, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sc;* repeat from * to * across. Ch 1, turn.
Repeat Rows 6-13 until you have a total of 118 rows. Then ch 1, turn, and do one more row of sc. Weave in ends, then move on to the border.
Border
Round 1: With off white, place one sc evenly around the entire blanket. Place 3 sc in each corner. *Tip: while working along the sides, place two sc for each colored row, so 2 sc per white row, 2 sc for each teal row, etc. This way you won't be adding unnecessary single crochets to the sides which could result in a wavy edge. Join with sl st to the first sc you made.
Finish off white, add teal.
Round 2: Ch 2, 1 hdc in each st around, placing 3 hdc in each corner. Join with a sl st to the first hdc. Finish off teal, add light blue.
Round 3: Ch 1, 1 sc in each st around, place 3 sc in each corner. Join with a sl st to the first sc. Finish off. Weave in ends.
