Recommended materials and tools:
YarnArt JEANS (55% cotton, 45% Polyacrylic)
Color No.:29
Filler
Spine
Hook : 2mm
Scissors
Second Glue
Black eyes – 2 pieces
Thin wire
Abbreviations
(US crochet terms):
ch – chain stitch
sc – single crochet
dc – double crochet
inc. – increase (2 crochets in 1 loop)
dec. – decrease (2 crochets together)
(….) - L times – repeat the sequence of
loops in brackets L times
Body:
1st row: Start 10 sc in a magic ring
2-4 th: 10 sc
5 th: 4 inc, 6 sc = 14 sc
6 th: (1 sc, inc) х 7 times = 21 sc
7-11 th: 21 sc
12 th: 2 dec, 10 sc = 12 sc
13 th: 12 sc
Stuff (it will only be in my head)
13 th: dec, 10 sc = 11 sc
14-83 th: 11 sc
84 th: dec, 9 sc = 10 sc
85 th: 10 sc
86 th: dec, 8 sc = 9 sc
87 th: 9 sc
88 th: dec, 7 sc = 8 sc
Next, insert the wire into the body, pre-bend the edges of the wire
89 th: dec, 6 sc = 7 sc
90 th: dec, 5 sc = 6 sc
91-92 th: 6 sc
Close 6 loops.
We glue the eyes and embroider the eyebrows.
English Crochet Pattern Snake
Final size of the toy with the recommended materials ~ 68 cm
Design by: Olga Kurchenko
Recommended materials and tools:
YarnArt Dolce Baby (100% Micro Polyester)
Color No.: 80361
Filler
Spine
Hook : 3mm
Scissors
Second Glue
Paperboard
Eyes – 2 pieces
Abbreviations
(US crochet terms):
ch – chain stitch
sc – single crochet
dc – double crochet
inc. – increase (2 crochets in 1 loop)
dec. – decrease (2 crochets together)
(….) - L times – repeat the sequence of
loops in brackets L times
Body:
1st row: Start 10 sc in a magic ring
2 th: (inc) х 6 times = 12 sc
3 th: 12 sc
4 th: (5 sc, inc) 2 times = 14 sc
5-6 th: 14 sc
7 th: inc, 5 sc, 3 inc, 3 sc, 2 inc = 20 sc
8 th: 3 inc, 5 sc, 3 inc, 9 sc = 26 sc
9-15 th: 26 sc
16 th: 2 dec, 10 sc, 2 dec, 8 sc = 22 sc
Stuff
17 th: (4 sc, dec) х 3 times, 4 sc = 19 sc
18 th: (2 sc, dec) х 4 times, 3 sc = 15 sc
Stuff
Longer than the crochet 74 rows of 15 sc, gradually stuff into the whole body
93 th: dec, 13 sc = 14 sc
94 th: dec, 12 sc = 13 sc
95 th: 13 sc
96 th: dec, 11 sc = 12 sc
97 th: dec, 10 sc = 11 sc
98 th: 11 sc
99 th: dec, 9 sc = 10 sc
100 th: dec, 8 sc = 9 sc
101 th: (1 sc, dec) х 3 times = 6 sc
102 th: 6 sc
Close 6 loops.
Gluing the eyes: I smeared the tips of the eyes with glue and inserted them in 10 rows from the beginning of the head, the
distance between the eyes is 10 loops. We embroider eyebrows over the eyes:
