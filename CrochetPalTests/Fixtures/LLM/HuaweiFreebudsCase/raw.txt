Huawei Freebuds Pro
airpods case
Design by Donglai
Materials:
Hook: 3mm
Yarn: Drops paris
Needle and thread for sewing eyes,
nose…
Abbreviations:
MR: magic ring
Sc: single crochet
Inc: Increase
St: Stitch
Slst: slip stitch
BLO: back loop only
FLO: front loop only
Top case:
Yellow:
Chain 11, turn and crochet to the second chain
from hook
1: 9sc, 4sc in the next st, 8sc, 3sc the the last
st ( 24)
2: Inc, 8sc, 4inc, 8sc, 3inc (32)
3: FLO 13sc, 2inc, 14sc, 2inc, 1sc (36)
4: 14sc, inc, 17sc, inc, 3sc (38)
5: 38sc (38)
Change white
6-9: 38sc (38)
Finish, cut and hide the yarn.
Top case:
Chain 11 and do the same with bottom
case but R3 do not FLO.
R5-7: 38sc (38)
Do not cut the yarn, crochet 2sc more
and join with the bottom case on the
next step.
To join To connect 2 shells, use
10slst. FLO from the top shell,
BLO from bottom shell.
Ears:
7sc in MR,do not join,
just tie MR
Arms:
1: 5sc in MR (5)
2-3: 5sc (5)
Crochet on both side
2sc
Sew all parts, nose, eyes and
cheeks to finish.
Hope you like it!
